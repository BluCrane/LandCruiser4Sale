Vehicle History

This Land Cruiser was bought in 2011 from the sole previous owner who had the vehicle specially converted for overlanding by 4x4 MegaWorld in Johannesburg. Since then it has been fitted with several high-end accessories with a total replacement value in excess of R150,000.

Since then the vehicle has been scrupulously maintained and comes with a full service record, as well as a recent Tectyl anti-rust coating.

The Land Cruiser was never put under excessive strain through unnecessary 'cowboy-style' off-road driving and has an accident free history. That is remarkable considering it has done five trips to the Kgalagadi, a few to the Wild Coast and a 14,000 km journey through Namibia, Botswana, Zambia and Zimbabwe. And not even a single punctured tyre!

Its reliability and ability to tackle difficult terrain with ease made such an impact that the Land Cruiser became a central character in a published travelogue of one of our journeys. You can read about it here.
